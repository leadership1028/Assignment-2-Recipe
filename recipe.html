Chicken Tikka Recipe

Ingredient

  • 500g boneless chicken (cut into cubes)
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp red chili powder
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil

Method:

  1. Marinate the chicken: In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, garam masala, lemon juice, salt, and oil. Add the chicken and coat well. Let it marinate for 30-60 minutes.
  2. Cook: Heat a pan or grill with oil. Cook the marinated chicken until golden brown and cooked through (about 8-10 minutes).
  3. Serve: Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with naan or rice.
